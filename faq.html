FREQUently asked questions

HOW MUCH EFFORT IS IT FOR ME?

As a business owner the topics you need to take care of are immense. That is why we want to create the far opposite of another burden for you and rather want to make it as easy and fast as possible. There is no need for constantly staying online in order to answer messages, watch ratings or to share pictures. Instead you engage with your customers through a fully automated system that provides your recommendations to your customers in the city they are currently at. Once you created your profile, connected with your customers and set up your recommendations, alas select your favorite places, you have completed all necessary steps. Another strong asset you have are your baristas, who are already the central point of contact for your customers in your store. Leverage their knowledge and have them share their favorite places through your businesses profile or encourage your customers to connect with you on Avantoria. 

WHAT IS IN IT FOR ME?

Avantoria is dedicated to creating a community. A community of enthusiasts with a passion for superior, unique experiences. You will finally be able to shape the experience of your customers beyond your place by complementing their journey with a marvelous discovery. This will bind your customers to you and increase their loyalty. 
In turn, other businesses you already have an established relationship with or who have had the chance to enjoy the offering, will recommend you to their customers. Your store traffic will increase with, even better, the audience you are targeting. 

how do you ensure there are no fake or unqualified ratings?

We don't believe in the rating system of current engines, and decided to eliminate this option entirely. Nevertheless with the unbelievable amount of places per city, recommendations (not ratings) help us narrow down our search. Yet it is the valuable recommendations that make for a wonderful experience. 

That is why we came up with the recommendation system from enthusiasts to enthusiasts. We let the customers decide whom they want to seek recommendations from, because we think it is the individual who can decide best whose feedback really provides value. So how do we ensure there are no fake or unqualified ratings? We are eliminating that function. entirely.
