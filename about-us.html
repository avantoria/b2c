About us 

Our mission. 

HoW IT all started

We love to travel and to discover the world, and we equally love superior coffee ahd food experiences. So whenever we are traveling, we are researching online to find the best small businesses representing the local culture and offering the superior quality that we are used to from our favorite shops in our hometown. Yet the current recommendation systems do not really facilitate this discovery, as the collection of places comes completely unfiltered, and the ratings come from foreigners who may have a completely different taste. Even though we spend massive amounts of time trying to find the local gems it is more likely that we cannot find what we are hoping for than being completely delighted.

We thought that there has to be a better way of recommendation systems providing actual value to businesses and consumers. We at Avantoria believe that If there is one group who knows great places offering a superior quality and splendid experience, then these are the business owners themselves. If we would receive a list of favorites from our preferred business in every city that we are visiting, there is no doubt that we will be delighted at all times. 
Our mission is to create a community of passionate business owners and enthusiastic customers to ensure an experience of consistent superior quality no matter where they are.
